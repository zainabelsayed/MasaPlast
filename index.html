ABOUT OUR COMPANY-MASAPLAST FOR PLASTIC INDUSTRIES

MasaPlast company has been established since 2009 specializes in plastic recycling industries in the local market of Egypt in terms of providing clever solutions through plastic recycling, especially polyethylenehigh & low density used in multiple applications particularly in the areas of packaging bags and soillining to decrease water absorption as per modern technologies applications, and membranes used in the fields of insulation and lining and so on.

MasaPlast provides its services at the highest levels of precision, efficiency and commitment owing to our belief in the need to contribute the promotion of productive projects related to industry, agriculture and the environment.

Within ashort period of time,MasaPlast becomes trustful for our customers inparticular through a policy of studying customer needs before and after their production and work together to remove any obstacles, we strive keep development and innovation in order to serve better.
