Our Vision, Mission & Commitments

    Our Vision

  • Excellence and competitiveness.
  • Effectively participating in clean environment through recycling.
  • Creating new jobs in the local market.
  • Export to neighboring countries in the continent of Africa.
  • We aim to deliver excellence everytime, to be a recognized between leaders companies as well as valued contributor to Egyptian economic and social progress wherever we operate.

    Our mission and commitments

  • We are committed to excellence customer service and as prompt demanded, more over delivering high quality products that does more reliable.
  • All team members are committed to Work hard looking for lead in plastic recycling field.
  • Continue to deliver innovate and creative solutions to improve work processes, in order to create and deliver the best value.

    Our Policy

    Our company policy is based on the following:

  • Development of environmental protection and improving occupational health and safety the top priority.
  • The company is committed to identify and approach the planning and implementation of their goals.
  • The need for compliance with laws and regulations concerning the activity of our company.
  • Work on the improvement of system performance.
  • Improving the efficiency of energy use.
  • Analyze potential risk management in a way that prevents the root causes of accidents.
